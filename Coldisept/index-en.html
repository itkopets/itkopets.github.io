A FEW FACTS ABOUT
nanoSILVER​

Today nanotechnology is actively used in medicine, as it opens up new possibilities in the treatment of various diseases.4-5

Nanotechnology is considered the "key technology of the 21st century6

Given the antibacterial and antiviral properties of nanomaterials, they are most often used as an alternative to antibiotics and antiviral drugs.7-12

Some of the most common nanomaterials that use in medicine are silver and copper.6

The antibacterial properties of silver and copper are known and proven for many decades. Technologies that allow the use of nanoparticles of these metals are changing approaches to the treatment of some diseases.4-12

Coldisept contains silver nanoparticles in the form of nanoplates, which allows them to easily adhere to various surfaces, including bacteria and viruses.14

Coldisept silver nanoparticles together with Chondrus crispus extract* create a protective gel layer that prevents the penetration of infection.13

The protective layer not only stops viruses and bacteria, but also stimulates the regeneration of the mucous membrane.13

Through silver nanoparticles, Coldisept nanoSilver can be considered as an alternative to standard methods of treating bacterial and viral ENT infections.4-12
