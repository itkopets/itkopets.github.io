Treating a child’s cold

Unfortunately, it is impossible to protect completely your child from the common cold for the whole life. Especially when your child every day is surrounded by his/her peers. It is enough for one child in kindergarten or at school to get sick, and the infection quickly spreads further. A child’s illness often entails sick leave of his/her mother. And here the questions arise: "How to treat a cold quickly? What is the best medicine"

The greatest number of a child’s colds occurs between the ages of six months and 12 years, when the immune system is being formed. Effective treatment of children with cold and ARVI requires immediate response to the first signs:

Feeling sick, headache;

Drowsiness or sleep disturbance;

Decreased appetite;

Deterioration of mood, moodiness;

Pain and sore throat;

Chills and fever;

Rhinitis - watery discharge at the beginning, which also flows down the back wall of the pharynx; in the future - a feeling of nasal congestion and violation of nasal breathing, violation of osmesis, sneezing¹;

In the cold season, it is needed to maintain the child’s immunity so that he/she does not get sick or recover faster. How can we do it safely? After all, it is advisable to use only natural and proven products for children.
